Israel Tay Nii Ashitey


Israel is the second Executive Director and Human Resource Manager of OPC Ghana. He is a learned mechanical engineer, and works as the local project coordinator as well as a mechanic and driver. Israel recognizes the beauty of all cultures and traditions, and yearns to preserve them and to help the people around him. He believes in energy and balance, loves nature and all its elements, and therefore protects, defends and uplifts all, without discrimination of gender, colour or race, let alone religious or social background. His core competencies are human resource management, mechanics, driving and spiritual philosophy.
